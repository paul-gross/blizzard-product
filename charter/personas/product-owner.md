# `persona:product-owner` — the product owner

## Who they are

They own *whether what was built is right*. Where the product manager decides order, this person holds intent: they are the natural first responder when the fleet has a product question, and the acceptance check on what it delivers.

## What their work looks like

The fleet's questions find them away from a desk more often than at one. An agent halfway through a feature hits an ambiguity a spec never covered — should the export include archived records? — and the answer exists only in this person's head. The product's job is to make that exchange cost seconds, not context switches.

## What they need from blizzard

- **Questions reach them where they are.** An agent's question about product behavior arrives on their phone with one-tap answers.
- **Their answer demonstrably lands.** "Delivered, agent resumed" — they see the loop close, not just their half of it.
- **Finished work is traceable and inspectable.** Every delivery traces back to its originating issue, and is reviewable at whatever gates are configured — a pull request when the review gate is on, the merged diff otherwise.

## What serves them

The ask/answer protocol end to end, the operator role, and the chat bot.
